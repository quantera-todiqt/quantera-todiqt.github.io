				<h2>Device-Independent Quantum Technologies</h2>
				<p>
					Device-independent (DI) approaches to quantum information processing provide
					security and performance guarantees that rely only on observed measurement
					statistics and the laws of quantum mechanics — not on trusting the inner
					workings of the devices. Our consortium pushes the frontiers of DI quantum
					key distribution, randomness generation, self-testing, and network protocols.
				</p>
